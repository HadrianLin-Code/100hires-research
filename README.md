# Research Project

## Research Topic: YouTube Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

[This research explores how successful creators and founders build YouTube content strategies specifically relevant to B2B SaaS audiences - covering content planning, audience building, and practical execution.]

---

## Selection Criteria

[I selected these 10 creators based on:
- Active, consistent YouTube presence
- Genuine practitioners - founders, operators or creators who've built real audiences/businesses not just commentators
- A mix of perspectives: bootstrapped SaaS (Rob Walling, TK Kader), scaled operators (Dan Martell, Alex Hormozi), founder interviews (Starter Story, Nathan Latka), startup ecosystem (Y Combinator), content/community strategy specialists (Greg Isenberg), conference-level insights (SaaStr), and modern AI-adjacent content (Sabrina Ramonov)]
